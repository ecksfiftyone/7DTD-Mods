Adds more craftable storage to compliment Sams Storage Stuff.
  - Gas pumps - To store gas and oil. 
  - Beverage coolers - To store all things drinkable.
  - Hardened Chest - It looks cool in certain spaces.
  - Munitions Boxes - 4 color variants for storing Ammo and Explosives. 
  - Military Ammo Pallets - Different variants
  - Utility Carts - Different Colors, Empty, Full, and Janitor Carts Too!


All storage is 9x10.
Most storage items drop themselves on destroy, so you can destroy and place elsewhere.
